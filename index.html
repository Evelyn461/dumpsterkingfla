Frequently Asked Questions

What items are prohibited in your dumpsters?

Hazardous waste, flammable materials, tires, and certain electronics are not allowed. Contact us for a complete list.

What happens if I need another bin?

Call us and we’ll schedule a pickup and replacement right away often within 24 hours.

What size dumpster should I rent?

It depends on your project. We offer sizes from 10 to 40 yards and will recommend the right one based on your debris volume and material type.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Rental periods vary, but most customers keep them for 7 days. Extensions are available upon request.

Are hazardous materials allowed?

No. Items like batteries, asbestos, paint, and chemicals are prohibited. We’ll give you a complete prohibited items list before delivery.

Can I schedule same-day delivery?

Yes, same-day delivery is available for most orders placed before noon.
